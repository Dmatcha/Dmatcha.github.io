The Rules of DEANSand

A dreamer's guide to navigating the realm

Welcome, Dreamer

DEANSand is an interactive narrative experience where you explore a dreamscape through choices and interactions. There are no wrong answers, only different paths to discover.

1How to Play

Navigation

Begin your journey at the dreamer page, where a hexagonal grid awaits. Each hexagon represents a different aspect of the dream realm:

  • Friends: Meet potential allies who may aid your journey
  • Villains: Encounter adversaries who challenge your resolve
  • The Day: Explore the realm's bright, inspiring aspects
  • The Night: Discover the hidden depths of shadow and mystery
  • The World: Uncover the history and lore of the realm
  • The Rules: This very page - your guide to understanding

Making Choices

When you click on a hexagon, you'll be taken to that section's unique interface:

  • Card Selection: Choose from three options - pick wisely, as each reveals different information
  • Interactive Elements: Click on twinkling lights, timeline segments, or other highlighted areas
  • Reading & Discovery: Some sections reveal their secrets through time and attention

2Game Mechanics

The Dream Realm Philosophy

This is not a game of winning or losing, but of exploration and discovery. Every choice reveals new information, characters, and possibilities. There are no game-over states - only different stories waiting to be told.

Character Interactions

  • When you choose a character card, the unselected cards fade away as your focus narrows
  • The chosen card animates to show its importance in your story
  • Detailed character information appears, revealing personality, motivations, and potential quests
  • Each character offers a unique perspective on the realm's nature

Exploration Elements

  • The Golden Ring: Click various points to discover how different times of day affect the realm
  • The Timeline: Different colored sections represent different ages - click to explore historical events
  • Atmospheric Elements: Some experiences unfold over time (like the letter-by-letter reveal of night text)

3Story Elements

Friends & Allies

The characters you meet as Friends represent potential companions for your journey. Each has:

  • Unique personalities and motivations
  • Individual fears and desires
  • Special skills relevant to dream navigation
  • Main quests they hope to accomplish

Villains & Challenges

Your encounters with Villains are not necessarily about conflict, but about understanding:

  • Each foe has their own perspective and reasoning
  • Some may become unexpected allies when understood
  • Their powers and methods reveal different aspects of the dream realm's nature
  • Even antagonists harbor secrets and hidden depths

Day & Night

The cyclical nature of day and night represents the balance within the dream realm:

  • Day brings clarity, hope, and manifestation of dreams
  • Night offers wisdom, mystery, and transformation
  • Each time and season reveals different magical possibilities

4Your Journey

There is no final destination - the beauty of DEANSand lies in the journey itself. Return to any section at any time to discover new details or make different choices. Each exploration reveals new layers of the story.

Creating Your Legend

  • Your choices shape your understanding of the realm
  • Different character selections create different narrative threads
  • Combine knowledge from multiple sections to gain deeper insights
  • Share your discoveries with other dreamers for richer conversations

Exploration Tips

  • Take time to read all text - each character and lore piece contains meaningful information
  • Return to sections after gaining new knowledge - you may notice details you missed
  • Consider how different characters' stories might connect
  • There are no time limits - explore at your own pace

Remember

In the dream realm, imagination is the only limit. There are no right or wrong choices - only stories waiting to unfold. Embrace the mystery, enjoy the journey, and let the realm surprise you.
